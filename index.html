Martin's Aquarium

Meet the fish in my aquarium

  • Beta Fish:

    Beta Fish are around 2.25inches in size, are found in freshwater,
    and eat small insects and aquatic invertebrates! This is Bob the Beta.

  • Clownfish:

    • Clownfish are around 4.3inches, are found in coral reefs in the oceans,
    and eat plankton, algae, and small crustaceans! This is Marlin.

  • Tuna:

    • Tuna are around 7.9 feet, are found in tropical regions,
    and eat smaller fish, squid, and crustaceans. This is Tina.

    Caring for fish and their tanks

    • Maintain pH balance and all other chemical levels.
    • Monitor water temperatures, and change the water regulary so it is fresh.
    • Clean the tank and any structures for the fish.
    • Make sure you pick the right size tank for your fish friend!

  • Angelfish:

    • Angelfish are around 6inches, are found in rivers and lakes in South America,
    and eat algae, plankton, and small aquatic insects. This is Gill.

  • Salmon:

    • Salmon are around 20inches to 5ft, are found in freshwater rivers and the Pacific and Atlantic Oceans,
    and they eat smaller fish and insects. This is Sal.

  • Piranha:

    • Piranha are around 18inches, are found in South American rivers and lakes,
    and eat other fish and aquatic animals. This is Perry.

  • Guppy:

    • Guppy's are around 1.5inches, are found in freshwater streams and rivers in South America,
    and eat small aquatic insects and plankton. This is Gus.

  • Coral Trout:

    • Coral trout are around 31inches but vary by species, are found in coral reefs in the Pacific region,
    and eat small fish, crustaceans, and cephalopods. This is Carl.

  • Catfish:

    • Catfish are around 3.3feet, are found in freshwater lakes, rivers, and ponds in North America,
    and eat small fish, plant matter, and aquatic insects. This is Kit.
